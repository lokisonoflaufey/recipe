Steak Pizzaiola

Prep Time: 15 Min

Cook Time: 10 Min

Total Time: 30 Min

This take on Pizzaiola is brimming with Italian Flavors: Tomato, mozzarella, and oregano. This dish cooks all in one skillet

Ingredients

  • 4 small eye of round steaks
  • Coarse Salt
  • Ground Pepper
  • 1 tbsp. Olive Oil
  • 4 clove garlic
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp. Chopped fresh oregano (or 1 teaspoon dried)
  • 2 oz. Fresh Mozzarella

Directions

  1. Generously season the steaks on both sides with salt and pepper and heat the oil in a large skillet over high heat.
  2. Work in batches to prevent over crowding.
  3. Add steaks, sear until browned (about 1-2 minutes). Transfer to plate.
  4. Reduce heat to medium and add the garlic. Cook, stir until golden brown (about 1 min)
  5. Add tomatoes and oregano. Cook on high heat until the sauce is thickened.
  6. Scrape the pan with a wooden spoon to mix in the brown bits from the bottom of pan.
  7. Return the steaks, add any accumulated juices from plate to skillet. Cover and cook until steaks are just heaed through.
  8. To serve, transfer the steaks to plates, top with mozzarella and garnish with more oregano.

Easy Perfect Milkshake

Prep Time: 5 Min

Cook Time: None

Total Time: 5 Min

This Milkshake will bring back memories of countertop service and carhop days. Nothing is better than a perfect milkshake. You can make this easy treat at home!

Ingredients

  • 4 large scoops (about 1.5 cups) Vanilla Ice Cream
  • 1/4 cup Milk
  • Whipped Topping for Garnish
  • Sprinkles for Garnish
  • Maraschino Cherry on top, for Garnish

Directions

  1. In a blender, blend together ice cream and milk
  2. Pour into a glass and garnish with whipped topping, sprinkles and a cherry.
  3. Bonus: for make a head, place in freezer till ready to consume, let sit for 5 minutes and enjoy!

Watermelon Salad

Prep Time: 15 Min

Cook Time: None

Total Time: 15 Min

This salad combines juicy watermelon, tangy lemon juice, savory feta cheese, and sweet honey into one delicious bowl. Serve right away on a hot summer day for a cooling treat!

Ingredients

  • 1 seedless watermelon
  • 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 cups feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1 cup red onion, sliced
  • 1/4 cup basil leaves, chopped

Directions

  1. Cube watermelon, or use a melon baller to scoop chunks of watermelon. Place in a large bowl.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine the olive oil, lemon juice, honey, and salt.
  3. Add feta cheese, basil, and red onions to the mixture.
  4. Pour the mixture over the watermelon.
  5. Toss watermelon with the mixture and serve immediately!
